About Hocus Focus:

  • About:

  • Hocus Focus is app that is designed to help you manage and prioritize your tasks and help you stay motivated as well!
  • The default focus and break time set is 25 and 5 minutes (The classic pomodoro timer) respectively. You can edit this in the settings page. After focusing on your task for the session, we recommend you to take a break.
  • You can prioritise the list of your task in the task list and start working on it, a session at a time.
  • Our purpose is to help you get your task done while you stay motivated and feel less strained at the same time.

  • How To Use:

  • Create a list top to bottom, according to your priority.
  • Start your timer by clicking on the Focus button.
  • After starting your timer, start working on your task.
  • After the session is finished, take a break.
  • Once the break is done, start the timer again.
  • Repeat until your list of tasks is done.

  • Advantages of Hocus Focus:

  • This app makes it easier for you to get started on your tasks.
  • With regular breaks, you feel motivated to do your work.
  • When you have a list of tasks in front of you, it's easier for you to get started on your task. And you don't have to keep your to-do list in your memory.
  • Taking breaks reduces the stress and strain.
